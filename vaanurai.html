The problem

Official messages create fear when people cannot understand them.

A bank letter, insurance notice, government form, school circular, or legal-looking message can create anxiety. People are not only asking, “What language is this?” They are asking, “What should I do now?”

Today, people struggle silently.

!They depend on relatives or local agents to read English or complex official language.
!They may pay small amounts repeatedly just to understand ordinary letters.
!For serious documents, they may delay action and miss deadlines.
!Sometimes they ignore the message because it feels too confusing.

Vaanurai turns confusion into clarity.

✓Send a photo or message on WhatsApp.
✓Get a plain-language summary in your language.
✓See deadlines, risk level, and the next action to take.
✓Listen as a voice note when reading is difficult.
How it works

As simple as sending a WhatsApp message.

Privacy and respect by design.

Vaanurai should be built with simple privacy language, minimal document storage, and clear user consent. People will only trust this product if it feels safe for family documents.

Important disclaimer.

Vaanurai explains documents in plain language. It should not claim to be a lawyer, doctor, insurer, bank, or government authority. For serious matters, users should verify with the official institution or a professional.
